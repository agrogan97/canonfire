Cannon Game

On the next screen, you'll see a target surrounded by cannons. Each cannon changes the target in some way once it's fired.
Fire the cannons at the target until it matches the goal shape.
The goal shape is shown at the start of reach new round, and then hidden - so pay attention to it!
You can skip to the next round or restart the round (up to a max of 3 times) using the buttons on the display.
